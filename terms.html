Clause 1: Monthly Capital Contribution

Each Partner shall contribute a fixed sum of ₹500 (Rupees Five Hundred only) per month towards the Group Fund.

The amount of monthly contribution may be revised from time to time by mutual consent of all active partners.

Contributions shall be credited into the group’s designated bank account only.

Contributions must be made via online transfer, UPI, or bank deposit to ensure transparency and traceability.

Clause 2: Due Date for Contribution

The last date for payment of monthly contribution shall be the 5th day of every month.

Any contribution received after the 5th shall be treated as delayed.

Clause 3: Late Payment & Penalty

If a Partner fails to contribute by the due date, a late fee of ₹50 shall be levied for every 7 days of delay.

If a Partner fails to contribute for two consecutive months, their voting rights shall be suspended until all dues are cleared.

If a Partner defaults for three consecutive months, the remaining partners may, by majority vote, terminate their membership, and refund their existing capital balance after deducting applicable penalties and expenses.

Clause 4: Capital Account Maintenance

A separate capital account shall be maintained for each partner in the books of the group.

All monthly contributions, penalties, and withdrawals shall be recorded transparently.

Each partner shall have the right to view their capital balance upon request.

Clause 5: Management and Decision Structure

(a) Appointment of Managers

Two partners shall be selected as Managers at the time of formation.

The Managers shall be responsible for:

  • Executing investment transactions on behalf of the firm.
  • Maintaining financial records.
  • Ensuring timely reporting and compliance.

(b) Authority of Managers

The Managers shall make all investment decisions, routine operational decisions, and maintain transparency by informing all partners.

Managers must act jointly; no single Manager shall take unilateral investment decisions.

If a majority (≥60%) of partners oppose a decision, that decision shall be cancelled or revised as per majority direction.

(c) Change or Removal of Managers

Managers may be changed at any time through a majority vote (≥60%) of active partners.

Upon replacement, new Managers must assume charge of all records, documents, and passwords within 30 days.

Clause 6: Non-Contribution and Replacement

Any partner unable to contribute due to personal reasons must inform the group before the due date in writing (email/message).

If such absence continues beyond 3 months, remaining partners may:

  • Decide to replace the partner with a new member, or
  • Freeze their capital account until reactivation.

The new member shall join by contributing the current monthly rate plus any applicable joining amount decided by the group.

Clause 7: Withdrawals and Exits

A partner may withdraw from the group by giving 30 days’ written notice.

Their final settlement shall include:

  • Return of their capital contribution, plus
  • Their share of realized profits/losses,
  • Minus any pending dues, penalties or charges(May include anykind of tax or platform charges).

Withdrawals shall be processed within 60 days of approval by remaining partners.

Clause 8: Accounts and Record Keeping

Detailed financial records shall be maintained digitally through Manager.io or equivalent software.

Each partner’s capital account shall be recorded separately.

Partners may inspect their capital account at any time.

Clause 9: Reporting and Transparency

A monthly report shall be shared in the group by the 10th of every month, showing:

  • Total funds collected,
  • Each partner’s payment status,
  • Investment summary, and
  • Current value of assets.

An annual summary (balance sheet + investment performance) shall be presented at the ending of every year(Nov-Dec).

Each partner shall digitally or physically acknowledge receipt of the annual report.

Clause 10: Admission of New Partners

New members may be added with majority consent (≥60%) of existing partners.

The new partner shall sign an addendum agreeing to this deed’s terms.

Profit/loss ratios shall be updated accordingly.
